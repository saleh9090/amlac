# Modules

## Core Modules

### Buildings

Manage building records.

### Units

Manage rentable units such as flats and shops.

Fields include unit number, unit type, layout, floor, area, electricity account, water account, status, and notes.

### Tenants

Manage individual and company tenants.

Tenant ID field should be called ID/CR to support both personal ID and commercial registration number.

### Contracts

Manage rental agreements between tenants and units.

Contracts include start date, duration in months, automatic end date, rent amount, payment frequency, payment method, and status.

### Rent Payments

Track each unit's rent payment for a selected month and year (paid amount, payment date, note). This is a tracking sheet for units only — there is no separate payment history page; the Rent Payments page shows total due, total paid, and total outstanding for the selected building/month/year.

Supports unpaid, partial, and paid statuses.

### Expenses

Record building expenses by category. Categories are managed from the Expense Categories page instead of a fixed list.

### Income

Record income entries (date, amount, category, note). Categories are managed from the Income Categories page.

### Dashboard

Show key indicators such as occupancy, active contracts, unpaid rent, income, expenses, and net income.

### Reports

Generate reports for income, expenses, outstanding payments, contracts, occupancy, and tenant history.
